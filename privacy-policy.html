Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PDRRMO Lanao del Sur (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information in connection with the OpCen-AutoPost application.

1. Overview

OpCen-AutoPost is a system designed to monitor rainfall advisories, earthquakes, and other hazards, and to automatically send notifications to users and post relevant updates to an official Facebook Page.

2. Information We Collect

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It only processes publicly available hazard data and official advisory content for dissemination and situational awareness purposes.

3. Use of Data

The data processed by the app is limited to publicly released information from official sources such as PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, and other authorized government agencies. No personally identifiable information (PII) is gathered or transmitted.

4. Data Security

Although no personal data is collected, we implement reasonable technical measures to ensure that system operations remain secure and free from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may interact with third-party platforms such as Facebook for automated posting. These platforms are governed by their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them separately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
